Gibson Les Paul Electric Guitar

The Gibson Les Paul is an iconic electric guitar known for its rich sound and classic design. Introduced in the early 1950s, the Les Paul has become a staple in the music industry, favored by many legendary guitarists. With a solid mahogany body and carved maple top, it delivers a powerful and warm tone. The dual humbucking pickups contribute to its distinctive sound, making it a versatile choice for various music genres.
